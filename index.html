Our Story

A Serendipitous Journey

Gael and Shelby first met in 2018 and quickly became friends. However, life took them in different directions, and they didn't see each other for four years. When they finally reunited, their connection deepened, and the friendship they once had began to blossom into something more as their feelings for each other grew.

Our Journey Together

As time passed, Gael and Shelby's bond grew stronger. They faced challenges together, celebrated each other's successes, and created countless memories that they'll cherish forever.

From spontaneous road trips to cozy nights in, every experience has brought them closer and deepened their love for one another.
